About Me

Abdullah Al Siam is a cybersecurity professional and researcher specializing in AI-driven cybersecurity and threat intelligence. He holds a Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering (Cybersecurity) and began his research career at the Cyber Security Centre, Daffodil International University, focusing on AI-based threat detection and secure data processing. He has hands-on experience in SOC operations, SIEM-based security monitoring, incident investigation, and response, and has worked as a Cyber Security Engineer at Enterprise InfoSec Consultants (EIC). He is currently an Associate Security Operator in the Fintech Engineering department at Digital Payments Limited (Pathao Pay), contributing to the security of large-scale digital payment systems. He has authored over fifteen peer-reviewed research publications in leading international journals, including IEEE Access (Q1), and serves as a reviewer for the IEEE Access journal. His research interests include AI for cybersecurity, malicious IP detection, encrypted traffic analysis, and intrusion detection systems, with a focus on bridging real-world security operations and AI-driven cyber defense.
